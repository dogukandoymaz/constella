## 2. Information we collect

We designed Constella to require **no account and no sign-up**. We do not ask for your name, e-mail, phone number, or precise location. However, some information is processed automatically, mainly to show advertising and keep the App working.

### 2.1 Information stored only on your device (not sent to us)
The following data is stored **locally on your device** using standard on-device storage and is **not transmitted to us**:
- Your game progress (current level, furthest level reached)
- In-game virtual currency ("Stardust") and unlocked cosmetic items
- Settings and preferences (selected language, music/effects on-off and volume, daily streak, daily quest progress)

Deleting the App removes this local data from your device.

### 2.2 Information collected automatically for advertising and analytics
When you use the App, our advertising partner (Google AdMob) and its subprocessors may collect and process:
- **Device advertising identifiers** (e.g., Apple's Identifier for Advertisers / IDFA), where permitted
- **Device information** (device model, operating system version, language, region, network type)
- **App usage and interaction data** (ads viewed, taps, session information, crash and diagnostic data)
- **Approximate location** derived from your IP address (city/region level, not precise GPS)

We do **not** collect precise GPS location, your contacts, photos, microphone, or camera.

### 2.3 Purchases
If you make in-app purchases, the transaction is processed by **Apple** through the App Store. We do **not** receive or store your credit card number or payment details. We may receive confirmation that a purchase was completed.

## 4. Advertising and App Tracking Transparency (ATT)

The App is supported by advertising provided through **Google AdMob**.

- On iOS, before any tracking that uses your advertising identifier, we present Apple's **App Tracking Transparency (ATT)** prompt asking for your permission.
- **If you allow tracking**, you may be shown personalized (interest-based) ads.
- **If you decline**, you will still see ads, but they will be **non-personalized**.
- You can change your choice at any time in **iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking**, and you can reset or limit your advertising identifier in **iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Apple Advertising**.

Google's handling of advertising data is described in Google's Privacy Policy and "How Google uses information from sites or apps that use our services":
- https://policies.google.com/privacy
- https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites
